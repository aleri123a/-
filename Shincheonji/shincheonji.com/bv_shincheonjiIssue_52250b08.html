SCJ ISSUE

We accuse the Christian Council of Korea(CCK)!

2018-07-20ㅣ조회수 3,902

복사 http://www.shincheonji.com/bv_shincheonjiIssue_5225 http://www.shincheonji.com/bv_shincheonjiIssue_5225 복사


We accuse the Christian Council of Korea (CCK)!

 

  

We accuse the falsehood and corruption of the corrupted and obsolete traditional denomination of the Christian Council of Korea(CCK) to the people of our nation.

 


■ Christian Council of Korea is not a religious organisation.

 


In the articles of association, which prescribes the purpose of the establishment of CCK and its characteristics, it states “Let’s unite Christianity in Korea as one and shout one voice to the Government and Society”. By looking at this, it is clear that they are an organisation formed for political reasons and own benefits, and not a religious organisation.

 


The Presbyterian denomination, which is the majority of CCK, instigated a joint movement with other denominations supporting the Samsun Constitutional Amendment during the period when the government took control through a coup d’état. This CCK is now being involved in politics, ruling over the heads of politicians, taking control over the administration of Korea. They have put pressure on government offices and called on their congregation to file a malicious complaint, thus disrupting the construction of new temples of Shincheonji, Church of Jesus (Shincheonji church). To spread bad perception of Shincheonji church to the ordinary people, they have encouraged their congregation to make all kinds of abuse and slander.

 


They also had a plutocracy to sell and buy the position of the Chairman of CCK and even committed an act of brutality by anointing hands and appointing pastors for those who had never even been to a seminary, if they have paid money. This political and administrative involvement by CCK is a crime that violates the content of Article 20, Clause 2 of the Constitution, what states that "Religion and politics must be separate", and thereby violates the order of the Republic of Korea.

 

 


■ CCK had served other gods than God.

 


During the period when Korea was under the Japanese colonial rule, the Presbyterian Church of Calvinism worked as the informants of Japan who trampled and destroyed our country and people, and incited pastors from each denomination to worship and praise the Japanese god. This was a violation of the very first commandment from the Law of God. The evidence of this is the Goshin group that refused to worship the Japanese shrine at that time and came out from Presbyterianism. These are anti-religious acts that deny God and are crimes against the law of heaven.

 


 

■ CCK is an organisation that aggravates coercive conversion programs that violates human rights such as murder, violence, kidnapping and confinement.

 


Feeling threatened from the rapid growth of Shincheonji, Church of Jesus, CCK fabricated and spread lies to the people. Conversion pastors affiliated with the CCK have undertaken coercive conversion programs that violate human rights through violence, confinement and kidnapping.

 


Conversion pastors deceive and instigate the parents of youth by making them put plaster on the mouth of their children, put handcuffs on their hands and legs, lock them up in a room and forcefully ask their children to change their religion with lies full of personal insults. Also, the pastors use the parents to make their children forcefully defer their studies or work and put the blame on Shincheonji church with lies that Shincheonji church is instigating these actions.

 


From this coercive conversion process, there have already been two cases of murders of a woman and young adult congregation member of Shincheonji church. However, CCK has not taken any responsibility in regards to these murders.

 


Religious organizations and politicians from different parts the world are hosting rallies against the coercive conversion programs of CCK, and the United Nations headquarter and Washington D.C. have denunciated it as a disgraceful action towards the name of religion. Coercive conversion program of CCK is a criminal act that is against religion, a crime that violates Article 20, Clause 1 of the Constitution of the Republic of Korea, and therefore denies the Republic of Korea.

 


 

■ CCK is an organisation that makes lies about the word of Bible.

 


CCK does not have the word of God, but rather full of teachings of worldly matters and lies. The pastors from the CCK have distorted the New Covenant, which is the words of prophecy that will be fulfilled, promised by the blood of Jesus (Luke 22:14-20). They have changed the meaning of the New Covenant and preached lies to the people. Pastors affiliated to CCK insisted that people and buildings will be raptured into the sky as this is the time of end of ages, and gave false teachings that the 'Four Living Creatures (Rev 4 and 6)' in the Book of Revelation means 'Four Gospels', the 'Two Witnesses (Rev 11)' who stand before the Lord means ‘New Testament and Old Testament’, the '666 (Rev 13)' means ‘Barcode’, and the earth will be ended before we meet the 21st century.

 


Jesus said in Matthew 13 that two types of seeds were sown in the churches of Jesus, and he said that the seed is the word of God (Luke 8:11). Jesus said the seed of the word of truth from God becomes the sons of God, and the seed of the lies (weed) are the words of the devil and they become the sons of the devil (Matthew 13:38-39). Shincheonji church is correcting the wrong teachings of lies with the word of truth.

 


Being born of the seed of truth and becoming God's children and entering the Kingdom of Heaven is better than being born of the seed of lies and becoming the children of the devil and go to hell. CCK is sowing the seed of the devil, lies and making their congregation members become the sons of the devil and sending them all to hell. This is the heaviest of all sins and an act that is against religion.

 


CCK, which is a tradition, has become obsolete, corrupted and committed all kinds of sins. They distorted and made 200 lies about the word of prophecy (New Testament, Revelation) that God promised and fed these lies to their own congregation members. They stipulated the orthodox revealed word that Shincheonji church teaches as a false religious cult.

 


With this, Shincheonji church sent numerous letters to CCK to suggest taking a test of 100 questions from the book of prophecy in New Testament in order to distinguish who is truly the orthodox and who is truly the cult. We asked them to not cause any more confusion to our society and politics through the result of this test, however there has not been any pastor or theological professor from CCK who responded to our request. This is the evidence that there is no word of God in CCK, and ultimately they have been defeated by Shincheonji church.

 


 

■ CCK is an organisation that divides the people of our nation into two and is an anti-nation, anti-society organization that is against peace and unification, and should cease to exist.

 


God promised peace in the Bible (Isaiah 52:7), the angel spoke of peace (Luke 2:14), and even Jesus proclaimed peace (Luke 19:38-42). Upholding this will, Shincheonji church is also doing the work of peace.

 


40 years of religious dispute in Mindanao Philippines, was ended through the peace work of Shincheonji church and Mindanao is changing into the nation of peace. Soldiers voluntarily fuse the weapons into tools for daily living (sickle). Airport police started a campaign returning guns. Religious disputes among the world is being resolved and the religion is becoming one. Schools in each nation are being reborn as peace schools.

 


Current Presidents and Chief Justices of the Supreme Court of Albania, Kosovo and Palestine and United Nations ambassadors met the delegation of peace messengers. They are actively supporting and participating in our peace activities for world peace and cessation of wars. Many nations around the world saw the peace movement of Shincheonji church and have proclaimed that the Republic of Korea is the greatest nation, however, CCK is actively disrupting this peace movement.

 


Shincheonji church did volunteering activities, made the biggest hand print Korean national flag, proclaimed the Declaration of Unification of Korea and the Declaration of World Peace, held events for Memorial Day tributes for the spirit of the patriots and the souls of all the fallen heroes. However, CCK became one with Christian Broadcasting System (CBS) and disrupted the volunteering activities, protested for banning to keep the biggest hand print Korean national flag in Seoul National Cemetery, took out the memorial stone for Declaration of Unification of Korea, and disrupted the rent venues for the peace activities. Even though these actions of CCK are anti-nation and anti-society, they rather insult Shincheonji church as anti-nation, anti-society and hold a campaign called ‘Shincheonji Out'.

 


 

■ To all the people of our nation! Please do not be deceived by the lies of CCK, and religious broadcasting station CBS!

 


As the word of the Bible (Hebrew 8) says, CCK is obsolete, corrupted and aging to soon disappear.

 


CCK should not commit any more sins to the believers, and repent their actions of anti-nation, anti-society, anti-religion, they should disband voluntarily and come forward to the word of the Bible. That is the only way for them to live.

 


To all the people of our nation! We must all come to Shincheonji, Church of Jesus which is the New Heaven and New Earth, the New Nation and New People that God promised and created and put in our efforts to be born again of the seed of God, be harvested, be sealed and belong to 12 tribes of God’s Kingdom. We must know that blocking the way to come to Shincheonji church is an act of crime.

 


Let us believe in the word "Repent! The Kingdom of Heaven is near."

 


 

June 2018

 


Congregation of Shincheonji, Church of Jesus
